How I invest

Principles I invest by

  1. Pay myself first. Invest on a schedule, before anything gets spent.
  2. Compounders in the core, trades on the edge. A long-term sleeve I rarely touch; a smaller swing sleeve for active ideas.
  3. Automate the boring parts. Recurring contributions and tracking run themselves.
  4. Time in the market beats timing the market.
  5. Conviction over noise. Buy quality, then let it run.
  6. Cash is a position. Keep dry powder for when it counts.
